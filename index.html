About

Bioinformatics with biological context.

I am an MSc Eng in Bioinformatics and Systems Biology with an interest in cancer biology, multi-omics analysis, survival modelling, and the biological interpretation of high-dimensional data.

I enjoy working in the space where everything initially feels messy: many files, many hypotheses, many possible directions. My strength is turning that complexity into structure while keeping the biological question at the centre.

How I work

From chaos to structure.

Curious first

Structured when it matters

I turn messy analyses into reproducible workflows, interpretable outputs, and clear scientific narratives.

Biology-aware computation

I care about models and pipelines, but I care just as much about what the results mean biologically.
